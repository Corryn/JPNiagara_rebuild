Niagara Falls Camping Vacations

Looking for the perfect Niagara Falls vacation?  Jellystone Park offers a complete range of exciting family activities.  Camping in Niagara Falls, Canada has never been easier - or more fun.  You'll find Horseshoe Falls, Marineland, the Butterfly Conservatory and many other attractions just minutes down the road.   If you like sports, you'll enjoy hiking, cycling, fishing, or golfing at any of 40 nearby golf courses.  If sight-seeing is more your speed, drive to scenic Niagara-on-the-Lake, take a winery tour, or visit one of many regional museums and art galleries.

When you want a break from sight-seeing, Jellystone Park's on-site activities guarantee family fun.  There is a heated pool for the whole family plus a wading pool for younger children, an 18 hole mini-golf course, shuffle board, volleyball, bocce and basketball courts, and a movie theatre!  Kids won't have time to get bored when they check out our games room, playground, and daily supervised activities like wagon rides and scavenger hunts.

A Niagara Falls camping vacation is well within your reach.  So head to Jellystone Park, for the ultimate family-oriented Canadian camping experience this summer. Our on-line Reservation service makes booking your family vacation easier than ever, and if you want more Niagara Falls tourist information ask us first, to see if we can help.
