Niagara Falls Getaway Package

Looking for the perfect Niagara Falls Getaway?  Jellystone Park offers a complete range of exciting family activities.  Getaway Vacations in Niagara Falls, Canada has never been easier - or more fun.  Visit attractions such as the Horseshoe Falls, Marineland, the Butterfly Conservatory and many other attractions just minutes down the road.   Golf, hiking, cycling, fishing and more are all nearby and make for a fun-filled getaway for the whole family.  If sight-seeing is more your speed, drive to scenic Niagara-on-the-Lake, take a winery tour, or visit one of many regional museums and art galleries.

If you want a break from sight-seeing, our on-site activities guarantee family fun.  There is a heated pool for the whole family plus a wading pool for younger children, an 18 hole mini-golf course, shuffle board, volleyball, bocce and basketball courts, and a movie theatre! 

A Niagara Falls Getaway Package is well within your reach.  So head to Jellystone Park, for a memorable Canadian camping experience this summer. Set up a tent, bring your RV or stay in a cabin! Our on-line Reservation service makes booking your family vacation easier than ever, and if you want more getaway package for niagara falls information ask us first, to see if we can help.
