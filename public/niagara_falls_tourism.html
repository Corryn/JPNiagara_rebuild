Niagara Falls Tourism

Looking for a special time away with the kids?  Jellystone Park in Niagara Falls provides you with all the comforts of home in a setting of unparalleled beauty.  When you aren't out sight-seeing, golfing, or dining at a fine restaurant, you'll feel pampered because our facilities are designed with your family in mind - and that makes us the right choice for your Niagara Falls vacation.  We offer full service camping and well maintained, full service RV lots.  Our rate schedule allows you to book your campsite or trailer for nightly or weekly rental.  Cabins and cottages can be booked in three, four or seven days. 

Jellystone Park's spotless washrooms, shower and laundry facilities will make you feel at home and our on-site restaurant and mini-mart guarantee that mealtime planning is a breeze.  Our sports facilities include volleyball, basketball and bocce courts, a heated pool and a wading pool for toddlers.  The 18 hole mini-golf course, movie theatre and supervised programs mean that your family always has something to do. 

Our carefully planned facilities, beautiful scenery, and Niagara Falls tourist attractions make Jellystone Park an ideal place for your family vacation.  So when you're thinking of camping at Niagara Falls, count on us as your home away from home.
