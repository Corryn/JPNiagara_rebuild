Campsites are based on 2 adults and only one tent or RV unit and one car per site. Additional tents require an additional site. Extra adults $6.00 and extra children $4.00 (Ages 3-17). 5% sales tax will be applied on campsites prior to July 1st and 13% after July 1st. One night's deposit required on booking. Three (3) night minimum stay on summer holiday weekends..

Rustic Sites are suitable for tents and small popup trailers. RVs are not allowed on these sites. Standard & Deluxe30 sites have water sewer and electric hookup with both 15 & 30 amp outlets. Standard sites are smaller back-in sites that are generally farther from the facilities. They are suitable for popups, small trailers or RVs 25' or less. Deluxe30 sites are like standard sites but are larger and are closer to the facilities or in a preferred area of the park. Deluxe50 & Premium sites have water sewer and electric hookup with 15, 30 & 50 amp outlets. Deluxe50 sites are back-in sites that can accommodate larger RVs of 35' or less. Premium sites are extra large pull thru sites in a preferred area of the park. They can accommodate RVs of any size.

Off Season Dates: April 23 - June 9, Sept 7 - Oct 15

In Season Dates: June 10 - September 6

Peak Season Dates: May 21-23, May 28-30, June 24-July 6, July 16-Aug 8, Sept 3-6
