July 2013

June 28 - July 1 | Canada Day Weekend

Yogi is having a huge birthday bash for Canada! Join us for a patriotic weekend where we will be celebrating everything Canadian! There will be cake, candles and a dance on Saturday evening. Bring all your favourite Canadian gear for our parade to win a prize for the most patriotic Canadian.

July 4-8 | Star Spangled / Country Western Weekend

Yiieee Hawww! We'll be celebrating Independence Day with some good ol' fashioned country fun! Y'all brush up on your line dancing for our hoe down with Yogi and BooBoo at the Pavillion on Saturday night! Don't miss out on a special country performance by Yogi, BooBoo and Ranger Smith!

July 12-14 | Carnival Weekend

The big top has come to Jellystone Park and the carnival is setting up. Come out & play your favorite carnival games ($) and jump on the giant bouncers ($) with Yogi and Boo Boo on Saturday afternoon. A carnival wouldn't be complete without a ring toss, balloon burst, face painting, fortune teller and lots of prizes!

July 19-21 | Christmas in July Weekend

It's beginning to look a lot like Christmas! Parents may bring a gift for their children to be delivered by Yogi Claus ($20 max) on Saturday afternoon at the Rec Hall. There will be a Christmas parade, carolling and sleigh ride around the park! Don't miss out on the fun!

July 26-28 | Retro Weekend

We're shaking things up at Jellystone Park and having a totally groovy weekend. We'll be creating lava lamps, tie dye shirts ($) and our second annual Car Painting Fiasco! Don't forget to wear your favourite outfit from the past to our to our DJ dance on Saturday evening.

PLEASE NOTE: Weekday activities begin June 22nd and end September 2nd. Check our website for more detail on our great family programs! (Events are subject to change without notice.)
