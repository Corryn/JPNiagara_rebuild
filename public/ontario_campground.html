Jellystone Park: The Ontario Campground

You and your family will find Jellystone Park, Ontario, located just minutes down the road from magnificent Niagara Falls.  Niagara Falls is one of the world's best known waterfalls and straddles the Canadian and American International border.  Nicknamed the "Honeymoon capital of Canada", Niagara Falls tourism industry offers many attractions for young and old, including hiking, cycling, fine-dining, and shopping.

The Niagara Peninsula is an enchanted region filled with many exciting places to explore. In the immediate vicinity, you will find three national historic sites, as well as six state parks, plus museums and amusement parks.  Golfing and wine tasting are among the many activities you will enjoy in this part of the world.

When you travel to Niagara Falls, Canada, make sure to visit the scenic town of Niagara-On-The-Lake located at the mouth of the Niagara River.  It is rumoured to be one of Canada's prettiest towns, but it is also a town with enormous historical significance.  Niagara-on-the-Lake has evolved into a tourist centre with a thriving arts scene.

Camping in Niagara Falls at Jellystone Park is an affordable way to explore this holiday destination and it's a convenient getaway from the city.  This Ontario campground is less than two hours drive from Toronto, Hamilton and Rochester and only 30 minutes from nearby Buffalo!
