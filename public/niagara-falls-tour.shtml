Niagara Falls Tours & Attractions

Jellystone Niagara is the starting point for many great adventures in and around the Niagara Falls area. Jellystone Niagara offers shuttle service from the park to the Falls, and multiple tours of Niagara Falls.

Below are a few Niagara Falls Attractions available through Jellystone Niagara either seperately, or as one Niagara Falls tour package.


Journey Behind The Falls

Go far below the rim of the gorge and behind the Falls as you experience the awesome sights and thundering sounds of the Canadian Horseshoe Falls.

 

 

 

Maid Of The Mist

From the deck of your vessel, you'll gaze upwards at massive walls of water free-falling into the basin. Afterwards, relax at Lookout Point or enjoy the Maid of the Mist Marketplace.

 

 

 

White Water Walk

Take an elevator down to the river's edge where you'll get a closeup view of one of the wildest stretches of white water in the world.

 

Whirlpool Aero Car

Ride high above the Niagara River's mesmerizing and unforgettable whirlpool.

 

Butterfly Conservatory

The world's largest glass enclosed Butterfly Conservatory with over 2,000 free flying tropical butterflies. Be sure to stroll through the world famous Botanical Gardens after visiting the Butterfly Conservatory.
