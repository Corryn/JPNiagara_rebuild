Camping in Niagara Falls Campground

Imagine waking up in the morning to find yourself surrounded by sugar maples and ancient white cedars.  Breathtaking views from the Niagara escarpment and its many  natural attractions are just down the road from you.  Where should you go to make the most of Niagara's beautiful setting?  The campground at Jellystone Park in Niagara Falls is your family's answer for an excellent outdoor experience.

When planning a family vacation in Niagara Falls, camping is an excellent choice.   Jellystone Park offers a full service campground with spacious 80 foot RV sites. Our recent service upgrades to RV sites and other exciting redevelopments ensure that our facilities are top of the line.  While our flexible rates let you book your Niagra Falls vacation to suit your schedule, so can stay for the week or just for the night.

Just click on our web site's online Reservations page to get started.  From here you can book your campsite, rent a trailer or Bare Bones Cabin.  Our pull-down menu lets you  customize your service requirements, so we can see that all your needs are met.  To rent a Yogi Cottage or Boo Boo Cabin, call our 1-800 number.  If you have any questions or need help finding Niagara Falls tourist information please contact us at the numbers provided.
