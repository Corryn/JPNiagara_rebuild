August 2013

August 2-5 | Hawaiian Weekend (Civic Holiday Weekend)

Aloha Campers! Vacation with a Hawaiian feel at Jellystone Park. Learn how to hula dance, go coconut bowling, carve a watermelon, and make a lei. Show Yogi your hula outfit and dancing skills at the Hawaiian luau dance party Saturday evening. Don't forget your favourite Hawaiian clothes.

August 9-11 | Scare Factor Weekend

Join us at Jellystone Park for a sticky, stinky, and slimy good time – but be prepared to expect the unexpected. Enter your family in the Jellystone Park's SCARE Factor Family Challenge on Saturday. Kids are encouraged to enter Jellystone Park's 9th Annual SCARE Factor Food Challenge on Sunday (prizes available).

August 16-18 | Wet 'n' Wild Warriors Weekend

Don't forget your swimsuit! We'll be playing water games and competitions all weekend long! There will be relay races in the pool, water balloons and much more!

August 23-25 | "Bearlympics" Challenges

Compete against Yogi, BooBoo and Ranger Smith in Jellystone's first “Bearlympics” Challenge! Race against Ranger Smith in a picnic basket relay and compete with Yogi Bear in a sandwich eating contest!

August 30 - Sept 2 | Halloween Haunt (Labour Day Weekend)

Goblins, ghosts, and witches – Jellystone Park's gone spooky! Don't forget to bring your Halloween decorations and costumes for the kids, as we will be having a "best site" and "best costume" contest. There will be Trick or Treating Saturday afternoon, so bring enough candy for approximately 150 children. Join us Saturday evening for our "Monster Mash" dance party at the Pavilion.

PLEASE NOTE: Weekday activities begin June 22nd and end September 2nd. Check our website for more detail on our great family programs! (Events are subject to change without notice.)
